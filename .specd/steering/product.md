# Product — Personal Blog / Portfolio

## Product
A public Laravel 13 website and read-only JSON API for a senior backend engineer. The site introduces the owner, highlights current experience, and surfaces the latest articles in a minimal portfolio layout. The current repo already serves Laravel routes, a public API, and a basic web entry point; this work turns that baseline into a proper blog/portfolio experience.

## Users
- Visitors who want a quick introduction and recent writing.
- Recruiters or hiring managers who want proof of backend experience.
- The owner, who needs a simple place to publish articles and keep the profile current.

## Value / why it exists
It gives a fast, professional way to communicate expertise, recent thinking, and contact points without requiring a full CMS. The public site should feel intentional and highly readable, with the Nothing.tech design language from `BUILD_PROMPT.md`.

## Out of scope
- Authentication or admin dashboards
- Comments, likes, or social feeds
- Newsletter signup, search, or tag archives
- Multi-author publishing or complex CMS workflows
- Payments, memberships, or gated content
